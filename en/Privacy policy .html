Privacy policy

التمرير للأسفل

The Decision Support Center (the “Center”) is concerned with protecting the beneficiaries’ data and preserving their privacy by applying the necessary administrative controls and technical measures in accordance with what is mentioned in the relevant laws, regulations, decisions, and policies.

The privacy policy has been drafted to help beneficiaries know the nature and type of data collected through their use of the DSC website (the “website”) http://www.dsc.gov.sa/ , the purpose of collecting and processing it, and how to protect it

It is important for the beneficiary to be aware of how the Center uses his personal data and rights in accordance with the policies and regulations in force in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Your use of the Center's website for the first time, whether you are a registered user or not, means that you have seen and tacitly agreed to what is stated in the privacy policy, terms of use and other policies, and the continuous amendments thereof, according to the Center's own discretion, provided that your acceptance and approval of the amendments applies based on your continued use of the site.

Beneficiaries are encouraged to check the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use and other policies for any updates.
