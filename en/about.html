About DSC

Scroll Down

DSC’s direction is guided by Vision 2030. It reinforces the effectiveness of the decision-making processes relying on evidence-based best practices to support growth and development in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Vision

To be a pioneering entity in providing insights and recommendations to support the decision-making ecosystem.

Mission

To support the decision-making process and improve its effectiveness and efficiency across different areas in a timely and precise manner through scientific and practical means and mechanisms that ensure making and executing the appropriate decisions.
